Festival Event Branding

Project Description

Creation of a concept for a festival and convention for rhythm games. This includes logo design, an advertisment poster, and mockups of merchandise with uniform design and branding.

Contained in this project:
  • Unique logo design
  • Poster with information about the event
  • Mockups of potential merchandise indluing t-shirts, pins, gloves, lanyards, water bottles, and event passes

Design Process: Visual Research and Moodboards

This event is based around the subculture of rhythm games within the wider gaming community. Within just the genre of rhythm based games, there are many different styles and methods of playing, all of which I wanted to capture in my visual research.
