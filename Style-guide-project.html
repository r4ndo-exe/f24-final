Style Guide Design

Project Description

This style guide was based on the Warm Up America Foundation, a non-profit organization that collects and creates knitted and crocheted works for those in need of blankets and clothing. My project conists of a hypothetical rebrand for the organziation, and the rules and guidelines that come along with that redesign.

Contained in this project:
  • A complete style guide document
  • Logo design, and logo use guidelines
  • Color systems, including a primary and secondary pallete
  • Type systems, defined uses for specific types of typography, and specifc settings for paragraph styles
  • 12-column grid system for content layout
  • Defined use of illustrations, imagery, and collateral systems that match the above listed rules of the style guide

Design Process: Visual Research and Moodboards

When designing for a non profit, I found it important to visually explore ideas of community, comfort, warmth, and togetherness, as well as research the design and visual aesthetics of fiber arts, as the angle of donating handmade works is something that is unique to the foundation when compared to other non-profits.
